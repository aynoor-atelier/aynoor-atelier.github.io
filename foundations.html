VOLUME II

Six Foundations

Every institution stands upon principles before possessions.

✦

The Six Foundations define the institutional framework of Aynoor Atelier. Every custodial action, every private treaty, and every historical responsibility proceeds from these enduring foundations.

✦
I   Custody ▸

Custody places long-term stewardship above temporary ownership. Every cultural asset entrusted to Aynoor Atelier shall be preserved with continuity, discretion and institutional responsibility.

II   Provenance ▸

Every custodial transfer strengthens an authenticated historical record. Documented provenance protects authenticity, trust and historical continuity.

III   Stewardship ▸

Stewardship reflects the obligation to preserve, protect and responsibly guide heritage across generations.

IV   Private Treaty ▸

Meaningful custodial transfers are conducted through confidentiality, discretion and institutional trust. Every Private Treaty preserves the dignity of both the cultural asset and its custodian.

V   Continuity ▸

Every manuscript, artifact and document carries a continuous historical narrative. Our responsibility is to ensure that this chain remains complete, documented and uninterrupted.

VI   Legacy ▸

Legacy is not defined by possession. It is defined by what survives, what remains documented, and what future generations are able to inherit.
